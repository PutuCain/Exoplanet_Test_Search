# Exoplanet_Test_Search
Retrieves exoplanets and confirms whether the exoplanet lies within the Goldilocks' zone of its star or not
Derives information from the lighcurve database drovided by NASA and can similarly determine the orbital period of the exoplanet on the basis of its lightcurve
Uses a csv file to export the orbital period data from the notebook and import it onto the calculations file
Takes several parameters and expresses them in terms of universal constants to determine the habitability of the exoplanet 
Determines whether the atmospheric constituents of the exoplanet can sustain potential life forms or not
It uses numpy, matplotlib, lightkurve and pandas
